Complete Training Curriculum

Six comprehensive modules covering electromagnetic theory to practical VFD integration, designed to develop industry-ready engineering technologists through hands-on learning and real-world applications.

1

Electromechanical Energy Conversion Principles

Theoretical Foundation

Master the fundamental principles governing motor operation through Faraday's law and Lorentz force concepts.

  • Faraday's law: Induced current from flux variation
  • Lorentz force: Force generation in magnetic fields
  • Energy conversion mechanisms
  • Mathematical modeling and analysis
Learning Outcomes: Understand electromagnetic principles, calculate induced currents and forces
2

Induction Motor Theory & Analysis

Core Concepts

Comprehensive understanding of induction motor structure, operation, and performance characteristics.

  • Motor structure and key components
  • Rotating magnetic field generation
  • Slip concept and torque production
  • Equivalent circuit analysis
  • Efficiency and performance calculations
Learning Outcomes: Analyze motor performance, calculate torque and efficiency parameters
3

Finite Element Simulation (FEMM)

Hands-On Design

Professional-grade simulation skills using industry-standard software for motor design optimization.

  • FEMM software introduction and setup
  • Simulation preparation and meshing
  • Magnetic flux density analysis
  • Material property influence studies
  • Geometry optimization techniques
  • Winding configuration analysis
Learning Outcomes: Design and optimize motor parameters using FE analysis

Motor Manufacturing Process

Hands-On Manufacturing

Complete manufacturing experience from core preparation to final assembly using professional equipment.

  • Core lamination cutting and stacking
  • Precision winding assembly techniques
  • Rotor construction and balancing
  • Complete motor assembly process
  • Quality control and inspection
Learning Outcomes: Manufacture complete motors meeting industry standards

Drive Systems & Integration

Advanced Application

Master various motor starting methods and advanced VFD integration for real-world applications.

  • Direct drive methods: DOL, star-delta
  • Auto-transformer starting techniques
  • Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) operation
  • System commissioning and tuning
  • Performance optimization
Learning Outcomes: Commission and optimize motor-drive systems

  • IEEE testing and performance standards
  • Safety regulations and compliance
  • Quality assurance procedures
  • Documentation and certification
